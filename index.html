About Me

As an experienced EcoHomes, Code for Sustainable Homes and BREEAM Domestic Refurbishment consultant, I advise design teams on how to meet tough environmental standards in challenging circumstances.
My main focus is on complex dwelling refurbishments, the improved environmental footprint of Listed Buildings and dwellings in Conservation Areas.
In addition to my consultancy work in environmental certification, I also prepare Planning Sustainability Statements and advise on accessibility, Lifetime Homes, Environmental Management and Construction Management Plans for Listed Buildings and bespoke developments.
I am a Member of The Institution of Environmental Sciences and Chartered Environmentalist.
